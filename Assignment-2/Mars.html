Mars

Known as the Red Planet

Its reddish appearance is due to iron oxide, commonly known as rust, on its surface.

Thin Atmosphere

Mars has a thin atmosphere composed mostly of carbon dioxide, with traces of argon, nitrogen, and oxygen.

Surface Features

The planet features volcanoes, valleys, deserts, and polar ice caps. Olympus Mons, the largest volcano in the solar system, is located on Mars.

Possibility of Water

Evidence suggests that liquid water may flow intermittently on Mars' surface.

Mission

Numerous missions have been sent to Mars to study its geology.
