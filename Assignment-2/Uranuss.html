Uranus

Tilted Axis

It has a dramatic axial tilt of about 98 degrees, meaning it rotates on its side, leading to extreme seasonal changes.

Atmosphere

Composed mainly of hydrogen, helium, and methane, giving it a blue-green color due to methane's absorption of red light.

Rings

It has a faint ring system, consisting of 13 known rings.

Moons

Uranus has 27 known moons, with names inspired by characters from Shakespeare and Alexander Pope's works.

Cold Temperatures

It has the coldest planetary atmosphere in the Solar System, with temperatures dropping to -224°C (-371°F).
