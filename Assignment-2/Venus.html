Venus

Thick Atmosphere
Venus has a very thick and toxic atmosphere composed mainly of carbon dioxide, with clouds of sulfuric acid.

Hottest Planet
Despite not being the closest to the Sun, Venus is the hottest planet in our solar system due to its runaway greenhouse effect.

Retrograde Rotation
It rotates in the opposite direction to most planets in the solar system, meaning the Sun rises in the west and sets in the east.

Surface
The surface is rocky and barren with many volcanoes and craters.
