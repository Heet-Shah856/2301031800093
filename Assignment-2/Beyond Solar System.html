Beyond Solar System

  • Star Clusters

    Groups of stars like globular clusters and open clusters.

  • Galaxies

    Large collections of stars, gas, and dust. Types include spiral, elliptical, and irregular.

  • Exoplanets

    Planets orbiting stars outside our solar system, some in the habitable zone.

  • Interstellar Medium

    Space between stars filled with gas, dust, and cosmic rays.

  • Dark Matter & Dark Energy

    Mysterious components making up most of the universe's mass-energy content.

  • Black Holes

    Extremely dense objects with strong gravitational pull.
