Earth's Moon

Natural Satellite

The Moon is Earth's only natural satellite.

Surface Area

It's about 14.6 million square miles (37.9 million square kilometers), roughly 27% the size of Earth.

Formation

Believed to have formed about 4.5 billion years ago, likely from debris after a collision between Earth and a Mars-sized body.

Gravity

The Moon's gravity is about 1/6th of Earth's, so you'd weigh much less there.
