The Sun:

The Heart of the Solar System

Star:

The Sun is a G-type main-sequence star (G dwarf) in the Milky Way galaxy.

Size:

It has a diameter of about 1.39 million kilometers, roughly 109 times that of Earth.

Composition:

Primarily composed of hydrogen and helium with small amounts of other elements.

Age:

The Sun is approximately 4.6 billion years old.

Energy Production:

The Sun produces energy through nuclear fusion, converting hydrogen into helium.
