Dwarf Planets (Pluto)

Dwarf Planet: Small, icy world in the Kuiper Belt.
Discovery: Found in 1930 by Clyde Tombaugh. Size: About 2,377 km in diameter. Orbit: Takes 248 years to circle the Sun.
Surface: Ice and rock with possible underground ocean.
Moon: Five moons; Charon is the largest.
Atmosphere: Thin, mainly nitrogen and methane.
